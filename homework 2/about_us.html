NON TI PAGO is a small family-run business, specialising in quality and authentic Italian food products.

It was founded in 2002 by Anna and Stacey Clerk following in Anna’s father’s footsteps and passion for his home country and its culture.

We give you the authenticity of traditional Italian food while adding a modern twist to our dishes – carefully crafted to give your palate a sure treat.

Respect of tradition, love for good food and warm of the staff, will help you to enjoy the flavors and atmosphere of the typical “NON TI PAGO”

The Restaurant is divided into three rooms reachable through some wooden steps, characteristic of the premises of the old town of the early 20th: wood, marble and wrought iron are the essential elements of a place with a classic atmosphere and at the same time warm and welcoming.

The signature pastas are made in-house daily and are paired with the highest quality, locally sourced ingredients.

It is a celebrated neighborhood restaurant offering a reliably indulgent experience, whether for a special occasion or enjoying dinner with friends.

About Anna Clerk

Anna Clerk is a dreamer, mother of three children. A real self-made woman, here where the desires, seasoned with so much effort, can become reality.

Anna has been a pizza chef since she was 13 years old, her craft took her around the world and finally to Las Vegas: first on vacation, immediately after work.

After about three years in LA, she meets her husband Andrew, and her life takes off.

In January 2002, thanks to her, and with the help of her sister Stacey, and their father Giovanni, the NON TI PAGO project started.
